How the 3 stages of emotionally focused therapy can help you

The three stages used in emotionally focused therapy are:

  1. De-escalating, assessing problems and letting
  2. Restructuring Interactions with each other that meet each other's needs and validate each other's communication styles
  3. Integrating new skills, understandings and ackowledging previous habits or behavior that wasn't working
